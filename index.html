Who We Are

A small team with real local roots.

PeakHost is a new, locally-operated co-host service built specifically for BC's short-term rental market. We're not a big national company with a call centre — we're a small, hands-on team based right here in British Columbia.

We focus on a limited number of properties so every host gets our full attention. We're not here to overpromise — we're here to do a genuinely good job and build a reputation over time.

A note of honesty: We're a newer service, which means you'd be one of our early clients. In return, you get our complete focus, competitive pricing, and a co-host who has a real reason to work hard for you. We'd love to earn your trust.

What We Handle

The tasks that eat up your time.

You keep full ownership and visibility of your listing. We just take the daily workload off your plate.

💬
Guest Communication
We respond to every guest inquiry, check-in question, and mid-stay request — typically within an hour. Consistent, friendly communication is one of the biggest drivers of your rating.
🧹
Turnover Coordination
We coordinate with your cleaning team to make sure every handover goes smoothly. Scheduling, checklists, and a final check before guests arrive — so you never get a "wasn't clean" review.
📊
Pricing Adjustments
We monitor local demand and adjust your nightly rate to stay competitive. We won't promise magic revenue lifts — but we will make sure you're not underselling during peak weekends.
⭐
Review Management
We follow up with guests after checkout and respond to reviews promptly. Good reviews come from good experiences — we focus on creating those from the first message to the last.
📝
Listing Improvements
We review your listing title, description, and photos and suggest practical improvements. Small changes here can make a real difference in how often guests click — and book.
🔧
Maintenance Follow-Up
When guests flag a maintenance issue, we document it and make sure it gets to you clearly. We can also help coordinate with local tradespeople you already trust.
